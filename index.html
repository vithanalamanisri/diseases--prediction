🩺 About the Project — Disease Predictor

The Disease Predictor is an intelligent, web-based health assistant designed to help users identify potential diseases through a simple and interactive interface. By entering basic details such as symptoms, age, gender, and weight, the system analyzes the information and generates an accurate prediction of possible diseases at the click of a button.

The platform provides a detailed output that includes the predicted disease name, recommended allopathic medicines, Ayurvedic treatments, general medicines, and practical health advice. A confidence level is also displayed to indicate the system’s reliability and accuracy in matching the user’s symptoms.

This project is developed with the vision of promoting early health awareness and digital healthcare accessibility. It empowers users to gain quick insights into their health status, make informed decisions, and seek timely medical consultation. By integrating AI-driven logic with a user-friendly interface, the Disease Predictor aims to bridge the gap between technology and preventive healthcare, making health assessment simpler, faster, and smarter.

⚙ How It Works

Input Details

The user enters basic information such as symptoms, age, gender, and weight into the system.

Data Processing

The system analyses the entered details using a built-in AI-based symptom–disease database. It compares the input symptoms with known medical patterns.

Prediction Generation

Based on the matching results, the system predicts the most likely disease associated with the given symptoms.

Result Display

The output includes:

  • 🩺 Predicted Disease Name
  • 💊 Allopathic Medicines
  • 🌿 Ayurvedic Remedies
  • 🧘 General Medicines and Health Advice
  • 📊 Confidence Level showing prediction accuracy

User Guidance

The user can use the provided health information as a reference or awareness guide and is advised to consult a doctor for professional diagnosis and treatment.
